# Statement of Work

**Client:** Coastal Comfort HVAC

**Project:** Website Redesign & CRM Setup

---

## Project Overview

Coastal Comfort HVAC is replacing its current Wix website with a new WordPress site and setting up a basic CRM to better manage customer follow-ups and scheduling. The goals are to present Coastal Comfort as a more professional, established business, generate leads through the new website, and modernize the office side of the business so Karen has better tools to track customers and follow up after service.

This SOW covers the website redesign, the CRM setup on HubSpot Free, basic SMS/text capability for customer communication, and team training. A small monthly retainer for ongoing support after launch is included as an option.

---

## Scope of Work

### 1. Website Redesign (WordPress)

A new WordPress website to replace the existing Wix site, including:

- Home page
- Services page, with sub-pages for heating, cooling, maintenance, and install
- About page
- Contact page (with contact form routed to Karen and connected to the CRM)
- Service area page
- Blog (set up and ready for posts)
- Financing page (referencing the existing third-party payment plan provider)
- Display of Google reviews on the site
- Mobile-responsive design using existing logo and the blue/orange brand colors
- Use of existing photography where usable; new photos to be provided by Coastal Comfort if needed

The site will be hosted on our infrastructure. We'll handle migration from Wix and assist with locating the domain registrar and pointing the domain to the new site.

SEO beyond standard on-page basics (page titles, meta descriptions, clean structure) is not included in this SOW and can be discussed as a separate engagement.

### 2. CRM Setup (HubSpot Free)

- HubSpot Free account setup
- Contact import from existing spreadsheets and records
- Basic deal pipeline configured for Coastal Comfort's sales/service flow
- Website contact form connected to HubSpot so new leads land in the CRM and notify Karen
- Automated review request to customers after a completed job

### 3. SMS / Text Communication

Setup of a tool to allow Coastal Comfort to text customers for appointment reminders and post-service follow-up. Specific platform to be selected during kickoff based on best fit with HubSpot.

### 4. Training

Training for Dan, Karen, and the team on the CRM and the website (basic content updates). Format and length to be confirmed during the project.
